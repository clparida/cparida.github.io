About Me

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at the Leibniz Institute for Baltic Sea Research Warnemünde (IOW), Germany. Previously, I worked at Curtin University (Australia) and IISc Bangalore (India).

I completed my PhD in Oceanography from Berhampur University. My research focuses on bio-optical modelling of Case-II waters in the Bay of Bengal.

I have over 12 years of experience and nearly 500 days of onboard scientific cruise experience.
