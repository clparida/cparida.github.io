Who am I?

About Me

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at the Leibniz Institute for Baltic Sea Research Warnemünde (IOW), Germany. Before joining IOW, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Curtin University, Australia, and at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India. I received my doctoral degree in Oceanography from Berhampur University, Odisha, India. My doctoral research focused on the bio-optical modelling of Case-II waters in the Bay of Bengal. I have 12 years of experience in the field of ocean science. In addition, I have nearly 500 days of onboard experience, having participated in several national and international scientific cruise programs.
