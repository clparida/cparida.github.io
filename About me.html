About Me

I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Scholar at the Leibniz Institute for Baltic Sea Research Warnemünde (IOW), Germany. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Curtin University, Australia, and the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India. I received my PhD in Oceanography from Berhampur University, Odisha, India.

My research focuses on bio-optical modeling of Case-II waters in the Bay of Bengal. I have 12 years of experience in ocean science and nearly 500 days of onboard experience in national and international scientific cruises.
